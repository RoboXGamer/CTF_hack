CTF Writeup: Dynasty

Challenge Name: Dynasty
Category: OSINT / Artifact Research
Flag Format: MythX{...}

Challenge Description

The challenge provides a blurred image and description of an early civilization artifact with narrative scenes. The goal is to identify the artifact, its museum location, and its inventory number to construct the flag.



Solution

Step 1: Determine Artifact

- Reverse image search and keyword queries for “early civilization artifact with narrative scenes knife”.
- Identified as the Gebel el-Arak Knife (ivory + flint, ~3300 BCE).

Artifact components:
- gebel
- el
- arak
- knife

Step 2: Identify Museum

- OSINT research indicates the knife currently resides in the Musée du Louvre, Paris.

Museum components:
- musee
- du
- louvre

Step 3: Find Inventory Number

- Louvre collection entry for Gebel el-Arak Knife shows inventory number E 11517.
- Normalize for flag: e11517.

Step 4: Construct Flag

- Format: MythX{artifactpart1_artifactpart2_artifactpart3_artifactpart4_museumpart1_museumpart2_museumpart3_inventoryno}
- Final:

MythX{gebel_el_arak_knife_musee_du_louvre_e11517}



Result

- Flag derived: MythX{gebel_el_arak_knife_musee_du_louvre_e11517}.



Tools Used

- Reverse image search
- Web search (OSINT)
- Louvre collection database
